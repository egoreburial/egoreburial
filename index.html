ABOUT US

Celestial Meridian is post-hardcore/metalcore band founded in 2015 in Russia, Belgorod by ex-members of SHOKRAN, THE HYSTERIA and :TREMOR. United by a common idea to play something new - Egore Levakov and Sergey Androsov wrote couple demo songs and started to look for other members. After while, Alexi Millers joins a band and brings the main sound of a band. Sergey Raev was invited as a session vocalist for first single, but in a process of recording became a full-time member of a band.

In winter of 2015 band releases first single "December" from upcoming EP, which was well received by the audience. After while, in spring of 2016 EP "Seasons" was released and it not dissapointed all those who waited for it. Right now band is working on it's first album.
